# Plot Breakdown - Digital Death Dolls

## Intro

### Scene 1: The Order

- **Location**: Rachel's bedroom
- **Characters**: Rachel
- **Summary**: Rachel orders NinjaMaster 9000 and Viking Warrior toys online when a crow crashes through her window and dies on her laptop.

### Scene 2: The Burial

- **Location**: Backyard
- **Characters**: Rachel, Tommy
- **Summary**: Rachel buries the crow under the oak tree; Tommy discovers her and she lies about planting a flower.

## Act 1

### Scene 3: Research and Reflection

- **Location**: Living room couch
- **Characters**: Rachel
- **Summary**: Rachel researches bird behavior while drinking vodka and Red Bull, feeling drowsy and haunted by the crow incident. She drinks from her wine glass printed with a drunken crow and "Nevermore Sober!!" She reflects on her substance abuse and whether she's an addict.

### Scene 4: University Flashback - The Lab
- **Location**: University paleontology lab
- **Characters**: Rachel, David (senior lab coordinator), Dean, students
- **Summary**: Rachel drinks vodka from a water bottle at work; students and David cover for her during the dean's visit, but she's later put on leave.

### Scene 5: Nighttime Vigil
- **Location**: Tommy's doorway
- **Characters**: Rachel, Tommy (sleeping)
- **Summary**: Rachel watches Tommy sleep and reminisces about her late husband Captain Ethan Branson, who died in a drunken accident falling from a second-story window at Thanksgiving. She reflects on her past bulimia and current substance abuse, saying the word "Addict" aloud.

### Scene 6: First Attack
- **Location**: Tommy's bedroom, night
- **Characters**: Rachel, Tommy, NinjaMaster 9000
- **Summary**: A mechanical hum startles Rachel awake, her wine glass tipping over. She hears Tommy's screams as the Ninja stabs him with its plastic sword; she throws the toy out the window, locks it, and passes out.

## Act 2

### Scene 7: Aftermath
- **Location**: Bathroom
- **Summary**: Rachel sits against bathroom wall after vomiting, having sedated Tommy with crushed pills in his milk.

### Scene 8: Kitchen Ambush
- **Location**: Kitchen
- **Characters**: Rachel, NinjaMaster 9000
- **Summary**: The Ninja steals a kitchen knife and stabs Rachel's hand before she destroys it with a frying pan.

### Scene 9: The Trap
- **Location**: Stairs
- **Characters**: Rachel
- **Summary**: Rachel steps on a nail protruding from the stairs and collapses, hitting her head on the banister.

### Scene 10: Crawling to Safety
- **Location**: Stairs to Tommy's bedroom
- **Characters**: Rachel, Tommy (sleeping)
- **Summary**: Rachel drags herself upstairs to check on sedated Tommy and falls asleep beside him while the Viking watches from the closet.

## Act 3

### Scene 11: The Duel
- **Location**: Hallway
- **Characters**: Rachel, NinjaMaster 9000 (rebuilt), Viking Warrior
- **Summary**: Rachel watches the Viking defeat the rebuilt Ninja in brutal combat using kitchen knife and scissors.

### Scene 12: New Target
- **Location**: Tommy's bedroom
- **Characters**: Rachel, Tommy (unconscious), Viking Warrior
- **Summary**: The Viking targets Rachel; she grabs unconscious Tommy and flees to the living room.

### Scene 13: Fire and Glass
- **Location**: Living room
- **Characters**: Rachel, Tommy, Viking Warrior
- **Summary**: Rachel fights the Viking with a chair, it falls into the fish tank, and she burns it alive with vodka and fire.

### Scene 14: Wounds
- **Location**: Bathroom
- **Characters**: Rachel
- **Summary**: Rachel cleans and bandages her knife wound and foot injury with vodka and duct tape.

### Scene 15: Final Confrontation
- **Location**: Balcony
- **Characters**: Rachel, NinjaMaster 9000 (decapitated)
- **Summary**: The headless Ninja attacks Rachel on the balcony with a glass shard; they struggle and both fall over the railing.

## Aftermath

### Scene 16: Discovery
- **Location**: Apartment and street below
- **Characters**: Tommy, paramedics, police (off-page)
- **Summary**: Tommy wakes to sirens and sees his mother's body on the pavement below surrounded by the shattered Viking remains.
