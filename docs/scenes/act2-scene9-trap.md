# Scene 9: The Trap

**Location:** Stairs
**Characters:** [[rachel|Rachel]]
**Summary:** Heading upstairs to check on Tommy, Rachel steps on a long rusty nail — it punches through her bare foot's soft arch, scraping between metatarsal bones. At least three inches long. She tries to lift her foot but the nail scrapes bone; it tears free with a sickening pop, leaving a ragged, pulpy exit wound. She collapses, head slamming against the banister, and passes out.
