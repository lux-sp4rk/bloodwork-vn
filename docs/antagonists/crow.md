# The Crow (First Vessel)

**Role:** The entity's first vessel in the story — not the entity itself, but its *car*

**Appearance:** Single crow, crashes through Rachel's window while she's ordering the toys. Dies on her laptop, blood smearing the glowing product photos of the NinjaMaster and Viking Warrior. Eyes mirror the NinjaMaster's red glowing eyes. Black feathers, sharp beak, splayed wings.

**The crash:** Not random. The entity chose a bird because birds are naturally death-adjacent. But the bird itself is a vessel, not a character. When the glass killed it, the entity transferred.

**What happened:** The crow died on the window → entity vacated → transferred to the toys, which animated moments later.

**The feather after Ethan's fall:** Same principle. A crow feather was on the sill where Ethan fell — the entity was wearing a crow at that moment. It transferred when Ethan died, wore him for a year, then moved again when it found a better offer (Rachel's slow approach to the threshold).
