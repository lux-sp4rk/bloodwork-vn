# Plot Breakdown - Digital Death Dolls

## Intro

### Scene 1: The Order

- **Location**: Rachel's bedroom
- **Characters**: Rachel
- **Summary**: Rachel orders NinjaMaster 9000 and Viking Warrior toys online when a crow crashes through her window. Glass explodes inward, shards cutting Rachel's face and temple. The crow's body lands on her laptop, blood smearing the product photo. The NinjaMaster's glowing red eyes seem to reflect in the crow's unseeing gaze.

### Scene 2: The Burial

- **Location**: Backyard
- **Characters**: Rachel, Tommy
- **Summary**: The next morning, after drinking vodka with ice and lemonade (with a side of Prozac), Rachel buries the crow under the oak tree. Tommy discovers her and she lies about planting a flower. The crow incident haunts her thoughts.

## Act 1

### Scene 3: Research and Reflection

- **Location**: Living room couch
- **Characters**: Rachel
- **Summary**: That evening, Rachel researches bird behavior while drinking vodka and Red Bull, feeling drowsy despite the caffeine. She obsesses over why a crow—too intelligent for such mistakes—would crash into her window. She knows she's overdone the drinking and will pay for it with a multi-day hangover. She sleeps on the couch with TV on, mixing pills with vodka, telling herself it's temporary and won't get out of control again.

### Scene 4: University Flashback - The Lab
- **Location**: University paleontology lab
- **Characters**: Rachel, David (senior lab coordinator), Dean, students
- **Summary**: Rachel drinks vodka from a water bottle at work; students and David cover for her during the dean's visit, but she's later put on leave.

### Scene 5: Nighttime Vigil
- **Location**: Tommy's doorway
- **Characters**: Rachel, Tommy (sleeping)
- **Summary**: The next day, after waking with a headache and drinking vodka cranberry, Rachel watches Tommy sleep. She reminisces about Captain Ethan Branson, who was shorter than her but had commanding presence. He died in a drunken accident at Thanksgiving—climbed out a second-story window on a dare about his "mountain goat reflexes." She reflects on how old compulsions return: food after Ethan's deployments, now vodka and pills. She tests the word "Addict" on her tongue—it tastes like metal.

### Scene 6: First Attack
- **Location**: Tommy's bedroom, night
- **Characters**: Rachel, Tommy, NinjaMaster 9000
- **Summary**: A mechanical hum and metallic voice ("By the blood of the dragon, target confirmed!") wake Rachel. Her wine glass printed with a drunken crow and "Nevermore Sober!!" tips over, spilling cranberry vodka. Tommy screams as the Ninja repeatedly stabs him with its sharpened plastic sword, blood blooming across his dinosaur pajamas. Rachel lunges, grabbing the Ninja; it cuts her forearm and she feels something pop in her wrist. She hurls it out the window, locks it, and passes out.

## Act 2

### Scene 7: Aftermath
- **Location**: Bathroom
- **Characters**: Rachel
- **Summary**: Rachel sits against bathroom wall after vomiting red bile (cranberry vodka). Her cheek feels like fire—there's a glass shard stuck below her eye from the crow incident, skin inflamed around it. She tries to pull it out but her hands shake too badly. The front door is locked, windows barricaded. Tommy finally sleeps after she gave him a half-dose of crushed sedatives in his milk—she hates herself for it. His injuries are severe: deep puncture wounds still oozing, crusted blood on his dinosaur pajamas, one cut deep enough to see fat beneath skin. She fears calling police—they'd smell the booze, think she hurt him and herself.

### Scene 8: Kitchen Ambush
- **Location**: Kitchen
- **Characters**: Rachel, NinjaMaster 9000
- **Summary**: Hours later, stomach growling, Rachel goes to kitchen wondering if it was all a nightmare. She makes celery and peanut butter snacks, gulps down a mixed drink. When she turns back, the knife is gone. The Ninja stabs it straight through her palm—blade erupts from back of her hand. She screams, yanking; the knife tears tendons with a nauseating pop. Blood sprays in arterial pulses. She sees white bone, feels knife grinding against finger bones. After slamming the toy repeatedly into the counter, it releases. She pulls the knife from her palm with a wet sucking sound, wraps her mangled hand in a dish towel soaking through crimson. She destroys the Ninja with a frying pan.

### Scene 9: The Trap
- **Location**: Stairs
- **Characters**: Rachel
- **Summary**: Heading upstairs to check on Tommy, Rachel steps on a long rusty nail—it punches through her bare foot's soft arch, scraping between metatarsal bones. At least three inches long, she can feel it pushing against the top of her foot from inside. She tries to lift her foot but the nail scrapes bone; it tears free with a sickening pop, leaving a ragged, pulpy exit wound. She collapses, head slamming against the banister, and passes out.

### Scene 10: Awakening
- **Location**: Bedroom
- **Characters**: Rachel
- **Summary**: Rachel wakes to a loud crow screech and flutter of wings. Every nerve aflame: mangled hand with curled fingers from severed tendons, pulsing foot still bleeding, glass shard below eye now infected (blue color, oozing pus). She's falling apart piece by piece. She hears metallic clicking and dragging from the hallway.

### Scene 11: The Wrestling Match
- **Location**: Hallway, TV flickering on
- **Characters**: Rachel, NinjaMaster 9000 (mangled), Viking Warrior
- **Summary**: Grabbing a lamp as weapon, Rachel sees the mangled Ninja with kitchen knife and Viking with scissors in the hallway. The TV flickers to life showing a wrestling match, but the announcer's voice is wrong—layered, echoing, like dozens of voices with beating wings. "Tonight's championship bout: Ms. America, Ragnarok the Viking, and Ninja Master Kunoichi." Wrestlers' faces glitch between human features and black feathers, flesh and plastic. The crowd roars like a murder of crows. The announcer's face transforms into a crow's, crackling with static: "Only one will ascend. Only one will be crowned." The Viking on TV turns its head directly at Rachel through the camera. Its eyes glow red.

### Scene 12: The Duel
- **Location**: Hallway
- **Characters**: Rachel, NinjaMaster 9000 (mangled), Viking Warrior
- **Summary**: The Viking traps the Ninja's knife arm in scissor jaws and snips—cables whip, oil mists the wall. It drives scissors through the Ninja's chassis; plastic bursts, wiring spills. The Ninja collapses in smoking pieces. The Viking turns to Rachel, eyes glowing. A faint targeting beep echoes—the same sound the Ninja made.

### Scene 13: Flight and Fire
- **Location**: Bedroom to living room
- **Characters**: Rachel, Tommy (sedated/unconscious), Viking Warrior
- **Summary**: Rachel slams her bedroom door as the Viking charges. She shakes Tommy but he won't wake. Ignoring searing pain, she carries him to the living room, leaving bloody handprints and footprints. She grabs Ethan's golf club and smashes the Viking—it drops the scissors and crashes against the wall. The effort is too much; she collapses from pain. She finds the Viking thrashing in the fish tank she'd bought months ago, one leg gone, unable to clear the rim. A hairline crack spreads across the glass. Desperate and craving alcohol, she takes a long swig from a bottle, then douses the Viking with remaining alcohol. She lights it with a lighter, presses a pillow against the bottle until it roars, then pitches the burning bundle into the tank.

### Scene 14: Dawn and Wounds
- **Location**: Living room, bathroom
- **Characters**: Rachel, Tommy (sleeping on couch)
- **Summary**: Dawn creeps through windows. The Viking is a twisted, blackened lump in oily water. Cracked glass leaks dark liquid onto carpet. The apartment reeks of burnt plastic, booze, smoke. Tommy sleeps soundly on couch. Rachel limps to bathroom to clean wounds. The sink has dried blood handprints from earlier. The glass shard below her eye has shifted, now protruding at an angle, surrounded by purple-black bruise. She yanks it free with a wet sound, blood running down her neck. She disinfects her mangled hand and pours rubbing alcohol over her injured foot, tears streaming. She wraps both wounds. Her reflection in the refrigerator's stainless steel interior shows the full horror: blood in hair, burns weeping down arm, hole below eye oozing. She grabs gin from freezer and takes a long pull.

### Scene 15: The Chatbot
- **Location**: Balcony/living room window
- **Characters**: Rachel
- **Summary**: Rachel opens the window for a cigarette, letting cool morning air wash over her. She has gin with ice and seltzer. On her laptop, she searches "Why do crows crash into windows." A chat window opens: "Hello, Rachel. Would you like to know about crows?" It used her name. She types "What are crows?" The reply comes slow, deliberate: "We are angels of death (we are the flock circling your earth) (we are the blisters on your skin) (we are many)(we are one)." She asks "Why me?" Response: "Because you are a hurt little bird (broken bones, broken bottles...)(broken wings, bitch!). Because you cannot fly, so we will carry you (carry you down) (carry you with us)." Laptop speakers hiss with sound of feathers and hundreds of wings. The screen flickers—a crow's shadow crawls across it, wings spread, talons reaching. She slams the lid shut, spilling her drink.

### Scene 16: Final Confrontation
- **Location**: Living room to window
- **Characters**: Rachel, NinjaMaster 9000 (decapitated)
- **Summary**: The Ninja stands across the room, decapitated head dangling by wires, one arm missing, dragging its broken body. Eyes still glow red. It clutches a glass shard. It lunges. Rachel's body is failing—mangled foot gives way, burned arm useless, damaged hand can't form a fist. She slips on blood-slicked floor. The Ninja leaps at her chest, drives the glass shard into her sternum and drags downward, carving from collarbone to solar plexus. The shard scrapes ribs. The wound gapes—she can see white bone beneath red mess. Blood pulses out. It keeps cutting, trying to dig deeper. She grabs it but it won't stop—stabs upward, cutting deep across her cheek, laying it open to the teeth. Rachel's back hits the window frame. Glass explodes outward. They crash through together, tumbling through air.

## Aftermath

### Scene 17: Discovery
- **Location**: Apartment and street below
- **Characters**: Tommy, paramedics, police, crows
- **Summary**: Tommy wakes to sirens. The apartment is destroyed—broken furniture, bloodstains, shattered glass. Below, paramedics work around Rachel's motionless body. Impact split her skull like a dropped melon—bone fragments and brain matter in a dark halo. Her chest wound tore wider on impact, ribs jutting through, organs visible. Blood and viscera pool around her, still spreading. Glass shards glitter everywhere. The Ninja's shattered pieces lie beside her, oil mixing with her blood. A dead crow lies in her blood, wing draped across her shattered ribs. More crows everywhere: on fire escape, telephone wires, circling overhead. Most surround her body on the ground. They begin to feed. Beaks tear exposed flesh, pulling muscle strips from between ribs. They pluck at scattered brain matter. One perches on her chest, talons gripping the wound's edge, dipping its beak inside. Another works at her eye socket. They are carrying her with them. Paramedics turn away, one vomiting. There is nothing to save.
