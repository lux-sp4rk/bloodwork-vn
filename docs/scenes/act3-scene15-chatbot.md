# Scene 15: The Chatbot

**Location:** Balcony/living room window
**Characters:** [[rachel|Rachel]]
**Summary:** Rachel opens the window for a cigarette, letting cool morning air wash over her. She has gin with ice and seltzer. On her laptop, she searches "Why do crows crash into windows." A chat window opens: "Hello, Rachel. Would you like to know about crows?" The entity speaks through the laptop speakers — fragmented voices, dozens of dying at once. It tells her: "You are a hurt little bird. Because you cannot fly, so we will carry you." She slams the lid shut, spilling her drink.

**Key beats:**
- Opens window for a cigarette, cool morning air
- Gin with ice and seltzer
- Searches "Why do crows crash into windows"
- Chat window opens: "Hello, Rachel. Would you like to know about crows?" — it used her name
- She types "What are crows?" — reply: "We are angels of death (we are the flock circling your earth) (we are the blisters on your skin) (we are many)(we are one)."
- "Why me?" — "Because you are a hurt little bird (broken bones, broken bottles...)(broken wings, bitch!). Because you cannot fly, so we will carry you (carry you down) (carry you with us)."
- Laptop speakers hiss with sound of feathers and hundreds of wings
- Crow's shadow crawls across screen, wings spread, talons reaching
- She slams the lid shut, spilling her drink

**[[entity|Entity]] note:** The entity speaks through the laptop because the toys are connected to Rachel's home network. The fragmented plural voices are every death the entity has been adjacent to speaking simultaneously.
