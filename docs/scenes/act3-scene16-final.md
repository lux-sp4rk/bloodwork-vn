# Scene 16: Final Confrontation

**Location:** Living room to window
**Characters:** [[rachel|Rachel]], [[ninjamaster|NinjaMaster 9000]] (decapitated)
**Summary:** The Ninja stands across the room, decapitated head dangling by wires, one arm missing, dragging its broken body. Eyes still glow red. It clutches a glass shard. It lunges. Rachel's body is failing — mangled foot gives way, burned arm useless, damaged hand can't form a fist. She slips on blood-slicked floor. The Ninja drives the glass shard into her sternum and drags downward, carving from collarbone to solar plexus. She grabs it but it won't stop. She slams back against window frame. Glass explodes outward. They crash through together, tumbling through air.
