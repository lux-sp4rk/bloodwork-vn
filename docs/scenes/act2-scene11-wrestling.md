# Scene 11: The Wrestling Match

**Location:** Hallway, TV flickering on
**Characters:** [[rachel|Rachel]], [[ninjamaster|NinjaMaster 9000]] (mangled), [[viking|Viking Warrior]]
**Summary:** Rachel grabs a lamp as weapon. She sees the mangled Ninja with kitchen knife and Viking with scissors in the hallway. The TV flickers to life showing a wrestling match, but the announcer's voice is wrong — layered, echoing, like dozens of voices with beating wings. Wrestlers' faces glitch between human features and black feathers. The announcer's face transforms into a crow's. The Viking on TV turns its head directly at Rachel. Its eyes glow red.

**Key beats:**
- Grabs lamp as weapon
- Sees mangled Ninja with kitchen knife, Viking with scissors
- TV flickers to life — wrestling match, but announcer's voice wrong
- Layered voices, echoing, like dozens of voices with beating wings
- "Tonight's championship bout: Ms. America, Ragnarok the Viking, and Ninja Master Kunoichi."
- Wrestlers' faces glitch between human features and black feathers
- Announcer's face transforms into a crow's, crackling with static
- "Only one will ascend. Only one will be crowned."
- Viking on TV turns its head directly at Rachel through the camera. Eyes glow red.

**[[entity|Entity]] note:** The TV announcer is the entity wearing a broadcast signal — using the screen like a megaphone.
