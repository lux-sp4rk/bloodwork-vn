# Scene 1: The Order

**Location:** Rachel's bedroom
**Characters:** [[rachel|Rachel]]
**Script label:** `act1_the_order`

## Summary

Rachel orders NinjaMaster 9000 and Viking Warrior toys online when a crow crashes through her window. Glass explodes inward, shards cutting Rachel's face and temple. The crow's body lands on her laptop, blood smearing the product photo. The NinjaMaster's glowing red eyes seem to reflect in the crow's unseeing gaze.

## Key Beats

- Rachel clicks "Place Order" on the NinjaMaster 9000 and Viking Warrior
- Confirmation email dings at the exact moment the crow strikes
- Glass shards pepper Rachel's face and temple
- Crow's blood smears across the glowing product photo of the toys
- For a moment, the NinjaMaster's red eyes seem to reflect in the bird's unseeing gaze
