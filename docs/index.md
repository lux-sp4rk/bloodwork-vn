# Digital Death Dolls / Blood and Servos

> Horror visual novel about AI-powered toys that malfunction and attack a grieving mother and her son. Themes: grief, addiction, the price of bringing back the dead.

**Engine:** Ren'Py 8.x
**Format:** Kinetic Novel+ (mostly linear, 2-3 branch points)
**Status:** Act 1 in progress, Acts 2-3 outlined
**Repo:** [GitHub](https://github.com/lux-sp4rk/digital-death-dolls)

---

## Story

Rachel, a widowed paleontologist struggling with alcohol addiction, orders AI battle toys for her son Tommy. A crow crashes through her window at that exact moment — the toys arrive — and the nightmare begins.

The truth: Rachel's late husband Ethan found something on deployment. Brought it home. It followed.
