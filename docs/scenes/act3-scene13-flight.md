# Scene 13: Flight and Fire

**Location:** Bedroom to living room
**Characters:** [[rachel|Rachel]], [[tommy|Tommy]] (sedated/unconscious), [[viking|Viking Warrior]]
**Summary:** Rachel slams her bedroom door as the Viking charges. She shakes Tommy but he won't wake. Ignoring pain, she carries him to the living room, leaving bloody handprints and footprints. She grabs Ethan's golf club and smashes the Viking — it drops the scissors and crashes against the wall. She finds the Viking thrashing in the fish tank, one leg gone, unable to clear the rim. She douses it with alcohol, lights it, and burns it alive.

**Key beats:**
- Slams bedroom door as Viking charges
- Shakes Tommy — won't wake
- Carries him to living room, leaving bloody handprints and footprints
- Grabs Ethan's golf club — smashes Viking
- Viking drops scissors, crashes against wall
- Rachel collapses from pain

- Hairline crack spreads across glass
- Desperate, craves alcohol — takes a long swig
- Douses Viking with remaining alcohol
- Lights it with a lighter, presses pillow against bottle until it roars
- Pitches burning bundle into the tank
