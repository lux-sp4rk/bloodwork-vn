# Tommy

**Role:** Supporting — Rachel's son, 8 years old

**Physical:** Strong resemblance to his father Ethan. Wears dinosaur pajamas with T-rex prints.

**Key traits:**
- Smart enough to debug Minecraft mods, taught himself Python from YouTube, knows what a neural network is at a kid-level
- Anxious — stays home from school to watch Rachel, not afraid of school but afraid of what she'll do alone
- Notices everything about Rachel — the vodka, the pills, the Slack notifications she ignores
- Drew the NinjaMaster with red eyes before it came — some part of him recognizes the entity's shape
- Tried to power-cycle the toy when it moved wrong; his smarts fail against the supernatural

**Injuries:** Multiple stab wounds to chest and collarbone from NinjaMaster's sharpened plastic sword; deep puncture wounds still oozing; one cut deep enough to expose fat layer beneath skin.

**Arc:** Traumatized by toy attacks, sedated by mother with crushed pills in milk, orphaned by story's end.
